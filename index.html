About Us

Our farm shop is located between Edinburgh and Carlisle and offers a variety of fresh and locally sourced products. From jams and preserves to eggs and bacon, we have everything you need to make a delicious meal.

Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour. We also sell fresh vegetables grown in our garden, as well as hand-made chocolates, local honey and marmalade, homemade breads and cakes, and locally sourced fruit juices, wines, beers, and ciders.

Visit our small petting zoo, play park, and take a walk around our farm to see how we produce our fresh products.
